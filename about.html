More Than Ice. A Mission.

Born from a vision to bring joy, community, and premium flavors to Hampton Roads — and beyond.

Welcome to Icely + Friends! My name is George, the proud founder and owner behind this sweet little venture. Growing up, I always had a passion for bringing smiles to people's faces. It started with a simple water ice machine and a dream.

What began as a humble idea has blossomed into a growing catering business that serves not only water ice but also lemon smashers — a refreshing twist on lemonade that has quickly become a crowd favorite.

I started this journey with the belief that food is more than just nourishment — it's an experience, a memory, a reason to come together. When you see someone's eyes light up after their first taste of our freshly made water ice, or watch a child discover their new favorite flavor, that's the moment that makes it all worth it.

From birthday parties and corporate gatherings to school fundraisers and community events, we're here to make sure your special moments are sweeter and more refreshing than ever. Our menu is crafted with care, and every cup is made with the finest ingredients.
